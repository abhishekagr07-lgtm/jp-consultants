About Us

Valuations built on rigour and trust

J.P. Consultants is a valuation and civil engineering firm established in 1985. Led by Er. Jai Prakash Agrawal — Chartered Engineer, B.Tech (Civil), M.I.E., F.I.V. — he is a Government Approved Valuer for both Wealth-Tax and Income-Tax, registered with C.B.D.T., Ministry of Finance, Govt. of India.

With a Fellow Life Membership of The Institution of Valuers, New Delhi, and membership of the All India Valuers Association, Kanpur, Er. Agrawal brings unmatched credentials to every valuation assignment. We are empanelled Panel Valuers with 7 leading public sector banks.

How It Works

A clear, transparent process

01

Request Received

Bank or institution submits a valuation request with property details and borrower information.

02

Site Inspection

Our certified valuer conducts a physical inspection, photographs the property, and reviews legal documents.

03

Market Analysis

Comparable sales and rental data is analysed to arrive at a well-supported market value conclusion.

04

Report Delivery

A signed, bank-format valuation report is delivered — typically within 48–72 hours of inspection.
